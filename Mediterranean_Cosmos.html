It all starts today

Here we are at Mediterranean Cosmos in Greece, spending an autumnal afternoon in the place which treasures so many of my young adulthood stories. It is a place to gather with friends, enjoy delicious food, taste refreshing beverages and shop the latest drops of fall/winter '22 just before the temperature starts to drop.
Mediterranean Cosmos is conveniently located between two of my favourite places: Thessaloniki and Halkidiki. 
In Thessaloniki one could find an endless variety of restaurants, bars, clubs and markets. A city always vibrant and lively, powered by a high population of students and innovative creators, thirsty to learn more, do more, live more.
On the other side, Halkidiki, a peninsula so picturesque, that it is impossible to visit only ones. From the wonderful sandy beaches to the pine trees surrounding most settlements; numerous tavernas offering fresh produce and entertainment for all ages and preferences. And that one unique and most romantic moment of the day when the sun drops behind the rocks of the mount Olympus - it is bliss. Let's plan our next visit
